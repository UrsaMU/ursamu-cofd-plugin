combat modifiers  -- Complete attack modifier reference (CoFD core p.92).

Attack pool modifiers (add or subtract dice before rolling):

  Modifier                     Dice Change     Notes
  ---------------------        -----------     ----------------------------
  Aiming (ranged)              +1 per turn     Maximum +3; lost if Defense
                                               applied while aiming. Action
                                               costs your instant action.
  All-Out Attack               +2              Brawl or Weaponry only.
                                               Lose all Defense for the turn.
  Autofire short burst         +1              3 rounds, one target only.
  Autofire medium burst        +2              10 rounds, up to 3 targets.
                                               -1 per target after the first.
  Autofire long burst          +3              20 rounds, any number of
                                               targets (ST approval).
                                               -1 per target after the first.
  Charge                       --              Move 2x Speed + Brawl/Wpny.
                                               Lose all Defense for the turn.
  Offhand attack               -2              Non-dominant hand.
  Pulling a blow               --              Target gains +1 Defense; set
                                               a max damage cap in advance.
  Specified target             varies          See: help combat specified.

Range modifiers (ranged attacks only):
  Short range                  no penalty
  Medium range                 -1
  Long range                   -2
  Beyond long range            chance die only

Concealment (penalty to attacker's pool):
  Barely concealed             -1   (office chair)
  Partially concealed          -2   (behind car hood, torso visible)
  Substantially concealed      -3   (crouching behind car)
  Full cover                   --   subtract cover Durability from damage;
                                    if Durability > weapon mod, no damage.
  Firing from concealment      -1 less than protection received
                                    (substantially = -2 to fire back).

Prone target:
  Ranged or thrown attack      -2
  Close-combat attack          +2

Shooting into close combat (single shot):
  Per avoided combatant        -2
  Per grappling combatant      -4
  Does not apply to autofire.

Other situational modifiers:
  Surprised or immobilized target   Defense does not apply.
  Willpower spend                   +3 dice (or +2 to one Resistance
                                    trait for one roll).
  Firearms in close combat          target Defense raised by gun Size + 1.

See also: combat, combat specified, attack, grapple, dodge
