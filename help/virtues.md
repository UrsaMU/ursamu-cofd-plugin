virtues  -- The Virtue anchor in Chronicles of Darkness 2e.

A character's Virtue is her higher calling, the personality trait that
represents who she is when she is being her best. Acting on Virtue is
difficult; doing so restores all spent Willpower, once per scene.

Choose exactly one of the four canonical Virtues during chargen Stage 1
with  +cg/set virtue=<name>.  The name is case-insensitive; it is
stored on the sheet in canonical capitalization.

Catalog:

  Competitive   Pushes herself to excel and outpace her rivals through
                honest effort. Regain Willpower when she honestly
                competes with someone who outclasses her, for stakes
                that will hurt if she loses.

  Generous      Feels best when giving to others, expecting nothing
                back. Regain Willpower when he is deprived of an
                important resource or put at risk because of his
                Generous nature.

  Just          Driven by fairness; sticks her neck out so everyone
                gets what they deserve. Regain Willpower when she
                sets her own goals aside to see justice done.

  Loyal         Devoted to a person or group. Regain Willpower when
                refusing to act against the group's interests puts
                him at risk.

Notes:
  The word Competitive appears in both the Virtue and Vice lists.
  A character may choose it as one or the other, but not both.
