vices  -- The Vice anchor in Chronicles of Darkness 2e.

A character's Vice is her short-term comfort: the easy way out, the
personality trait she falls back on to dodge her real feelings. Acting
on Vice reinforces her sense of self even when self-destructive, and
restores one Willpower point, once per scene.

Choose exactly one of the four canonical Vices during chargen Stage 1
with  +cg/set vice=<name>.  The name is case-insensitive; it is stored
on the sheet in canonical capitalization.

Catalog:

  Ambitious     Wants advancement, rank, and authority beyond what she
                has earned. Regain Willpower when she jockeys for
                position instead of doing what is actually useful.

  Arrogant      Defines himself by being better than those around him.
                Regain Willpower when he lords his superiority over
                someone else.

  Competitive   Needs to win; may cheat to do so. Regain Willpower when
                she indulges in a competition that distracts from more
                pressing matters.

  Greedy        Hoards resources for himself in case they are useful
                someday. Regain Willpower when he takes an advantage
                for himself, denying it to another character.

Notes:
  The word Competitive appears in both the Virtue and Vice lists.
  A character may choose it as one or the other, but not both.
