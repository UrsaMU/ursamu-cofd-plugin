vitae costs  -- Common Vitae expenditures in Vampire: The Requiem 2e.

Standard costs:
  Effect                Vitae   Action
  --------------------  ------  ----------------------------
  Heal 1 bashing        1       reflexive
  Heal 1 lethal         1       1 turn each
  Heal 1 aggravated     5       1 day of rest + 1 Willpower
  Boost a Physical      1       +2 for the scene
  Blush of Life         1       one hour of warmth
  Wake at sunset        1       automatic each night
  Create a ghoul        1       per month of the Vinculum
  Power a Discipline    varies  see the Discipline entry

The Embrace:
  Drains all but 1 Vitae from the sire. The remaining point is
  returned at the moment of the childe's death.

Per-turn limits apply to all of the above. See:
  help vitae blood-potency

See also: vitae, touchstone, sheet
