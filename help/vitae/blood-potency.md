vitae blood-potency  -- Blood Potency reference table for Vampire: The
                       Requiem 2e.

Blood Potency table:
  BP  Max Vitae  Per-Turn  Min Humanity  Notes
  --  ---------  --------  ------------  ----------------------
   0    10          1          -         Newly Embraced.
   1    10          1          -         Animal, human, or vampire.
   2    11          2          -         Animal no longer slakes.
   3    12          3          -         Human or vampire.
   4    13          4          -         Human or vampire.
   5    14          5          7         Human blood weakening.
   6    15          6          6         Vice/Virtue or Kindred.
   7    20          7          5         Vampire vitae preferred.
   8    30          8          4         Must hunt Kindred.
   9    50          9          3         Almost only Kindred.
  10    75         10          2         Mythic monster.

Per-turn cap:
  The Per-Turn column is the maximum Vitae the vampire can spend in a
  single turn of fiction. +vitae/spend with a higher N still goes
  through but warns that the action spans multiple turns.

Minimum Humanity:
  The Min Humanity column is the Humanity floor enforced by Blood
  Potency. A vampire whose Humanity drops below the floor cannot
  raise BP further until Humanity is restored.
