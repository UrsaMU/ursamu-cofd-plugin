merit-combat  -- Combat-relevant merits wired into the dice engine.

Five merits modify combat rolls automatically when the relevant action
fires. You do not need to add their effects by hand; +roll, +attack, and
+combat/init pick them up from your sheet.

Merits:
  Fast Reflexes (1-3)
    +dots to your Initiative result. Applied during +combat/init.

  Quick Draw (1, instanced per weapon class)
    Drawing the equipped weapon does not consume your first action even
    on a Slow weapon, provided the instance matches. Examples:
      Quick Draw (Firearms)    suppresses Slow for guns.
      Quick Draw (Melee)       suppresses Slow for swords, axes, etc.

  Killer Instinct (1-3)
    +dots to attack pools against a target who is Beaten Down or has
    taken more than half their max Health in damage. Stacks with other
    pool modifiers.

  Iron Stamina (1-3)
    Reduces the dice penalty you take from your own wound levels by the
    dots you own, to a minimum of 0. Never converts a penalty into a
    bonus.

  Heavy Hitter (3, melee only)
    +1 to the raw hits of a successful melee attack before armor is
    subtracted. Does nothing for ranged attacks.

Storage keys (used by +sheet/set):
  fast reflexes
  killer instinct
  iron stamina
  heavy hitter
  quick draw:firearms     (one per weapon class)

See also: merits, attack, combat, roll
